About

Hi :) I am a graduate student in the lab of Dr. Kasie Raymann at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. My current research aims at understanding factors shaping gut-microbial community composition and resulting structural and functional microbiome dynamics in the humble honeybees.

When I am "free", I like experimenting with cooking and photography.

Research

I am interested in leveraging computational and molecular methods to understand host-microbiome relationships. My broad research interests entail microbial evolution, microbial-omics and big data in biology.

I believe that fusion of biology and computational prowess can and will lead to scientific wonders ...
