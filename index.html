Current Research

Bees are important pollinators and play an essential role in agricultural production worldwide. These bees harbor gut-microbial communities which contain a large repertoire of metabolic capabilities impacting both health and disease of the host. However, our knowledge of the microbial assembly and evolution is lacking. My current work thus aims to understand the factors shaping gut-microbial community composition and resulting structural and functional microbiome dynamics in the humble honeybees.
